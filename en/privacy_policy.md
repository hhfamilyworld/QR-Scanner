The QR Scanner app ("App") does not collect or transmit user data to external servers. This Privacy Policy explains what information is handled, how it is used, and how long it is retained.

---

## 1. Information We Handle

All data is processed locally on your device. Nothing is sent to the developer's servers.

**Stored on device only (never transmitted)**

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Scanned QR code / barcode content | Display scan results and save to history |
| App settings (theme, language, etc.) | Maintain user preferences |

**Processed when using specific features**

| Data | When | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| Contact information | When saving a contact from a QR scan | Save to device address book (not transmitted) |
| Location / Wi-Fi data | When connecting via a Wi-Fi QR code | Establish Wi-Fi connection (never stored or transmitted) |
| Advertising ID (GAID) | Upon ad consent | Serve personalized ads via Google AdMob |

---

## 2. App Permissions

| Permission | Required | Purpose |
|------------|----------|---------|
| Camera | Optional | Real-time QR code and barcode scanning |
| Photos / Gallery | Optional | Detect QR codes in saved images |
| Contacts | Optional | Save vCard QR contacts to address book |
| Location (NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES) | Optional | Connect to Wi-Fi networks via QR code |

Denying a permission only restricts the related feature; core app functionality remains available.

---

## 3. Data Retention

| Data | Retention Period |
|------|-----------------|
| Scan history | Until deleted by the user or the app is uninstalled |
| App settings | Deleted automatically when the app is uninstalled |
| Advertising ID | Until ad consent is revoked or the app is uninstalled |

Scan history is stored in a local database (SQLite) on your device only. No scan data is ever sent to or stored on the developer's servers.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following service to display advertisements.

**Google AdMob**

- Data Controller: Google LLC
- Data Collected: Advertising ID (only when ad consent is granted)
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

For users in the EEA (European Economic Area) and UK, a Google UMP (User Messaging Platform) consent form is shown on first launch to obtain your choice on ad personalization.

## 5. GDPR Notice (EEA & UK Users)

The developer complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR.

- **Withdraw Consent**: Go to App Settings → Ad Personalization to change your preference at any time.
- **Right to Erasure**: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.
- **Access / Correction / Deletion Requests**: Contact us at the address below.

| Processing Activity | Legal Basis |
|---------------------|-------------|
| On-device scan history | Legitimate interest (core app functionality) |
| Wi-Fi connection via QR | Legitimate interest (user-initiated feature) |
| Personalized ads | Consent (obtained via UMP form) |
| Non-personalized ads | Legitimate interest |
